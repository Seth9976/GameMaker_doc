Drawing And Miscellaneous

The following functions are additional tools for getting various different sets of data about a skeleton animation sprite:

  • skeleton_get_minmax
  • skeleton_get_num_bounds
  • skeleton_get_bounds

 

You can also use various draw functions designed specifically for these types of sprite (apart from the regular draw_sprite functions). These functions give you more control over what is being drawn and permit you to change animation specific features, such as timing and skins:

  • skeleton_collision_draw_set
  • draw_skeleton
  • draw_skeleton_instance
  • draw_skeleton_collision
  • draw_skeleton_time
  • draw_enable_skeleton_blendmodes
  • draw_get_enable_skeleton_blendmodes
  • draw_enable_skeleton_blend_override
  • draw_get_enable_skeleton_blend_override
